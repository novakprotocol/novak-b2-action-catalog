## Policy

This repository is currently published as a use-and-reference source, not as an open contribution project.

External pull requests, issues, feature requests, discussions, and unsolicited patches are not accepted at this time.

The project owner may reopen contribution channels later after a separate governance, review, and acceptance process exists.

## Why

The catalog contains support, safety, evidence, and operating-boundary logic. Changes must be reviewed under the project owner's release process before they are accepted.
